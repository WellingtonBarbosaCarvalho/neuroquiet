Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

At NeuroQuiet, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Make a purchase: name, email, shipping address, payment information
  • Create an account: username, password, preferences
  • Contact us: messages, feedback, questions
  • Subscribe to newsletter: email address

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Traffic source (referrer)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process orders: complete transactions, send confirmations, track shipments
  • Communication: respond to inquiries, send order updates
  • Marketing: send special offers and news (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Service improvement: analyze usage to enhance user experience
  • Security: prevent fraud and protect our systems
  • Legal compliance: meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third parties who help us operate:

  • ClickBank: payment processing
  • Shipping carriers: product delivery
  • Email providers: newsletter distribution
  • Analytics providers: Google Analytics for traffic analysis

Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with court orders or legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Protect user safety

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: receive your data in structured format
  • Object: oppose processing of your data
  • Restriction: limit processing under certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@neuroquiet.com

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure storage on protected servers
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular security monitoring

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve browsing experience
  • Display relevant ads

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
